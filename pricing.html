How does the Free Trial work?

Your first 14 days are free with all features. Near the end of the 14 days, you will get an email reminder. You can choose whether or not to continue with a plan that fits your number of Staff Members on your team or simply dis-continue use and your account will automatically go in-active.

How do I invite a Staff Member to this app?

It's as easy as logging in, clicking the "Invite User" option, entering the Staff Member's email and role (eg. Brand Ambassador, Chef, ect) and clicking "Send". After that, the Staff Member will receive an email with a special link to signup.

How do I change plans?

On your dashboard, there's a button that says "Plans". After clicking that, you'll be offered the option to switch plans.

How do I cancel my plan?

On your dashboard, there's a button that says "Plans". After clicking that, you'll be offered the option to cancel your plan.

Do these plans come with a contract or commitment?

Nope! You can begin a plan with no starting fees, and cancel your plan at any time with no cancellation fees.

What features are unlimited?

You get an unlimited amount of Events to list, unlimited email notifications, 24/7 Support, and depending on your plan, an unlimited amount invites for Staff Members.
