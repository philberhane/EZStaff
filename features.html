Features

The following features will give you a glimpse as to how our software works

Portal

Enjoy the simplicity of our Administrator Portal, where you have the option to Create an Event, Edit an Event, View All Events, or Invite a Staff Member to the app. After Creating an Event, you can now kick back and relax as our software performs Feature #2.

Automated Notifications

With our automated Email Notification Feature, your Staff Members will receive notifications everytime an Event is Created, as well as be given the option to Accept. You will also be notified whenever a Staff Member Accepts an Event, Checks into an Event, or Cancels an Event.

GPS Check-In

With our GeoLocation Feature, your Staff Members will have the option to check in after arriving at the venue. Our software will check their Current Location and only allow them to Check-In if they're at the designated location.
